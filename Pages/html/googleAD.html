Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our Google Ads services

What is Google Ads, and how does it work?

Google Ads is an online advertising platform developed by Google. It allows businesses to display brief advertisements, service offerings, product listings, or videos to web users. It works on a pay-per-click model, meaning you only pay when someone clicks on your ad.

How soon can I see results from Google Ads?

The timeline for seeing results can vary depending on factors such as your industry, competition, and budget. However, many businesses start seeing initial results within a few days to a couple of weeks after launching their campaigns.

How do you measure the success of Google Ads campaigns?

We measure success through various metrics including Click-Through Rate (CTR), Conversion Rate, Cost Per Click (CPC), Return on Ad Spend (ROAS), and Quality Score. We provide detailed reports and analytics to track these metrics and optimize campaign performance.
